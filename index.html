// research

I am currently working on my PhD under the guidance of Professor Qing Zhou. Our work centers around high-dimensional statistical computing and graphical models. We are developing efficient algorithms and theory for estimating large Bayesian networks in a high dimensional setting using techniques from sparse approximation and compressed sensing.

Some of my general research interests include:

  • Machine learning / graphical modeling / unsupervised learning
  • Computational statistics / distributed learning / high performance computing
  • High-dimensional statistics / compressed sensing
  • Sparse modeling / low-rank approximation / inverse problems
  • Bayesian networks / causal inference
  • Decision theory / social choice theory
